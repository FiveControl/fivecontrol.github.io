Rules

We also have rules, read these so you don't get restricted from using our services! Read below

General

Respectful Conduct: Users must maintain a respectful demeanor while interacting with the bot and other users. - Punishment: A warning for the first offense. Continued violations may result in a temporary mute or cooldown period for using the bot's features.

Appropriate Content: Users should refrain from generating or sharing inappropriate or offensive content through the bot. - Punishment: Immediate restriction from using certain bot features for a specified period. Repeated offenses may lead to a temporary or permanent ban from using the bot.

Mindful Usage: Users are expected to use the bot's features responsibly and avoid spamming or overusing commands. - Punishment: Graduated penalties, starting with a temporary cooldown on bot commands and escalating to a temporary or permanent ban for repeated violations.

Moderate

No Abuse of Features: Users must not exploit or abuse the bot's commands or features for malicious purposes or to disrupt server activities. - Punishment: Immediate suspension of bot access for a specified period, with the possibility of a permanent ban for severe or repeated infractions.

Respect for Guidelines: Users are required to adhere to any specific usage guidelines or instructions provided by the bot's developers or administrators. - Punishment: Suspension of bot access, with the duration determined by the severity of the violation and the user's history of compliance.

No Unauthorized Actions: Users should refrain from attempting to manipulate or interfere with the bot's functionality or operations without proper authorization. - Punishment: Immediate and permanent revocation of bot access, with potential further action depending on the nature of the attempted breach.

Severe

Zero Tolerance for Harassment: Any form of harassment, bullying, or discriminatory behavior towards other users or the bot itself will not be tolerated. - Punishment: Immediate and permanent ban from using the bot, with potential reporting to Discord Trust & Safety for further action.

\nProhibition of Illegal Activities: Users must not engage in any illegal activities or use the bot to facilitate illegal actions. - Punishment: Immediate and permanent ban from using the bot, with potential reporting to Discord Trust & Safety and relevant authorities.

\nProtection of Bot Integrity: Users are prohibited from attempting to exploit, hack, or compromise the security or integrity of the bot's systems or data. - Punishment: Immediate and permanent ban from using the bot, with potential legal consequences depending on the severity of the breach.
